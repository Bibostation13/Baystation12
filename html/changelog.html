03 June 2021

Ilysen updated:

  • Added a whole smorgasbord of new augment options, which you can obtain in a variety of ways.
  • Traitors can access an Augments section in their uplink to buy CBMs - compact bionic modules that can be used to self-install augments with no medical skill. They are, however, very painful to use.
  • Added an Augments section to character loadout for minor augments.
  • Added a selection of fluff augments that do nothing mechanically, and exist purely for roleplay purposes. You can get them from roundstart loadouts.
  • Integrated HUD augments can now be picked at roundstart by appropriate roles.
  • Some augments can now be discovered by grab-inspecting the body part they're on.
  • Medical records now differentiate between roundstart prosthetics and roundstart augments.
  • Hidden augments are now fully hidden on body scanners instead of showing on the "internal organs" list as a mechanical prosthetic.
  • HUD augments now let you control medical status/security status/etc when examining another person.

CrimsonShrike updated:

  • Mobs that can drown will not drown if there is not enough fluid on turf above to allow swimming, if they are able to overcome gravity.

02 May 2021

updated:

  • Fix border windows, window click-through and enforce window construction steps.
  • Fix mineral processor eviscerating mobs.
  • IC signalers no longer require refreshing a pin to work after printing.
  • Full-tile windows can only be anchored to floor/low wall/grille/non-dense objects.

Cupax3 updated:

  • The Deck Chief has dug up an old space piano from one of the Entertainers and put it up for grabs in the Commissary!

Domic2 updated:

  • Adds pipe sparkers, pipe fires can now be started without heating the mix. Can be controlled via signalers and buttons, or disabled with a screwdriver.

Mucker updated:

  • Fix the Depth Scanner's UI not updating on button clicks.
  • Updates the Depth Scanner, Anomaly Power Utilizer, Artifact Scanner, Artifact Harvester, Suspension Field Generator, and the Reagent Grinder UI's to use modern browsers.
  • Fix mined exoplanet mineral turfs not having gravity.
  • Lowered the amount of quicksand on desert planets.

NL208 updated:

  • Makes all modular scanners repairable with nanopaste or cable.

Paprikue updated:

  • Syringes now use the current transfer amount for taking blood samples instead of filling the whole thing immediately.
  • Computer modules will now consume power as originally intended.
  • Computers now die when you remove their batteries (as long as they don't have a tesla link to directly power them)
  • Tweaked battery depletion rates so that they don't discharge almost immediately.

30 April 2021

updated:

  • Using adrenaline to start a stopped heart now does half as much damage.

0sj/Jaydn updated:

  • Skrellships doors are now named

Cupax3 updated:

  • The Caulship now has three new cat walks beneath the shield generator and Ascent SMES, allowing for tinkering with the power net directly.
  • Both Silicons and Organics now use !; or ,2.h to use languages and emotes over radio.
  • Holopads no longer have phantom conversations and connections due to people calling when you're on a call.
  • Four suits were slightly adjusted in size to match with accessories.

Mucker updated:

  • Search results in VV will remain in view after clicking 'refresh'.

SealCure updated:

  • You now split thaler stacks using your other hand, just as you do for material stacks.

29 April 2021

updated:

  • The cyclopes now has ~70% less fuel in the pipes, to prevent roundstart explosions.
  • Adds two EMT belts to skrellship medbay.

CrimsonShrike updated:

  • Jumping into deep water is no longer harmful.
  • Water allows overcoming of gravity so long you can swim.
  • You will no longer sink as long as water is deep enough.

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Sensor consoles will now print scans in the language that makes sense for their mapped areas.
  • Modular console printers can now have their printing language configured in the 'Computer Configuration Tool' menu for the console. These languages are limited to what languages the user knows.
  • A `set_language()` proc has been added to papers to allow for easier changing of a paper's language without dealing with VV, which likes to crash clients when editing the language var for some reason.
  • AI-controlled zombies are no longer permanently comatose.

21 April 2021

BlueNexus updated:

  • Temperature should have an increased impact on thrust, and burn mode works again.

CrimsonShrike updated:

  • Exosuits no longer accumulate radiation. They will instead return radiation at current location reduced by armour directly.

Mucker updated:

  • EMPs now don't blind you if your HUD or goggles are deactivated. Security HUDs also don't protect from flashes if they're not active.
